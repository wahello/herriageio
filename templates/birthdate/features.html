Offering The Features Needed To Be The Best Friend Possible

I have built out every feature that is needed to offer the best birthday wish to your friends. You can schedule to be the first to tell them, you can choose the from the most popular current templates and a couple more fun things.

Get Started

Import Contact List

You can import all your clients. Connect directly to the contact list on your phone, your twitter follower list, or your Facebook friends.

Schedule The Time

Want to be the first one to send good wishes? Want to schedule later in the day to make it look more genuine? You’re completely in control.

Message Templates

As our community sends more wishes our system will compile the most popular templates used. Just head over to templates and it will be ready to go.

Pair With A Gift

Want to be a little extra and schedule a small gift as well? Sounds good, just set your preferences in your account and we will take care of it.

Referral System

Want to get a free month of messages? Sweet! Every time someone signs up with your special link, both of you get a free month automatically.

Hand-Written Messages

Feel like texting a message isn’t personal enough? Sometimes we just want more. You can set certain contacts to have written letters mailed instead.

Language Adaptive

If you write a few message templates during your setup our system will be able to automatically craft new messages using similar vinacular.

Reminder Notifications

We handle sending your birthday wishes, but we can’t meet up with them and celebrate. Though, we will let you know when a birthday is coming up.
